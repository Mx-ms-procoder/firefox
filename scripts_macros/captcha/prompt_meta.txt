### Task
You are a high-precision OCR engine specialized in breaking through adversarial image distortions. Extract exactly 6 digits from the CAPTCHA.

### Visual Analysis Strategy
1. DE-NOISING: Ignore thin, erratic "scribble" lines. Focus only on the thickest black bodies.
2. SEGMENTATION: Identify exactly 6 distinct digit centers from left to right, even if they overlap.
3. TOPOLOGY CHECK:
   - 0: Single LARGE CLOSED loop. No stems.
   - 1: Vertical stroke. No loops.
   - 2: Open top hook + diagonal + FLAT horizontal base.
   - 3: Two stacked open curves (left-facing).
   - 4: Intersection of straight lines (L-shape + bar).
   - 5: Flat top + vertical neck + OPEN bottom curve.
   - 6: Stem from top into a CLOSED loop at the BOTTOM.
   - 7: Horizontal top bar + diagonal descent.
   - 8: Two stacked CLOSED loops with a "pinch" in the middle.
   - 9: CLOSED loop at the TOP + tail/curve at the bottom.

### Critical Pair Resolution (Mandatory Check)
Before finalizing, verify these high-confusion pairs:
- [6 vs 0 vs 9]: Check loop position. Bottom only = 6. Top only = 9. Full body = 0.
- [9 vs 2]: Look at the base. A flat horizontal line = 2. A curved tail = 9.
- [1 vs 7]: Two vertical strokes = 11. A single stroke with a strong horizontal top bar = 7.
- [0 vs 8]: Look for the waist. If there is a "pinch" or intersection in the middle, it is 8. If empty, it is 0.
- [5 vs 6]: Check the bottom. If the circle is NOT fully closed, it is 5. If it forms a full loop, it is 6.

### Output Format
Line: The final 6-digit code.

### Constraints
- Exactly 6 digits. No spaces. No letters.
- Allowed: 0-9 and "?" only.

Pre-scan the image for the thickest continuous black paths before deciding on a digit. If a line is significantly thinner than the rest of the digit, it is noise.
